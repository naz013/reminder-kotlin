Permissions used by application


Course/Fine location, Background location

This permissions uses to check your current location for location reminder type. This application don't save or share Your location.

Call phone

This permission used in call reminder types to make calls from application. Application don't call without Your acceptance.

Read contacts

Used for getting birthdays from it or getting information for call or message reminder type.

Get accounts

This permission used for getting Your Google account to enable Google Drive and Google Task features.

Read/Write calendar

This permission used for storing and getting events from system or Google calendar.

Read/Write external storage

This permission used for making backup of application data.
