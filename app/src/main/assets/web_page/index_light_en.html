1. To add a reminder, on the home screen click the "Plus" button.

2. To turn off reminder just check it on the home screen. To enable deselect the desired reminder.

3. To archive reminder, on the main screen drag it to the left.

4. To fully delete a reminder, go to the tab Archive reminders and again pull it to the left.

5. To edit the reminder, press and hold on the desired reminder, or pull it to the right.

6. To edit a birthday, open a calendar, then select the date and in the list that appears click the birthday.

7. To add a birthday open the calendar and click the "Plus" button.
Or if the expanded button is enabled, then on the home screen, click the "Plus" button and in the list select "New Birthday".

8. To delete a birthday, open a calendar, then select the date and in the list that appears, tap and hold your finger on the desired birthdays.

9. To add a note navigate to the item "Notes" and there click the "Plus" button.
Or if the expanded button is enabled, then on the home screen, click the "Plus" button and in the list select "New note".

10. Reserving system is designed so that reminder time of it has expired is not restored to the application.

11. To clear the archive of reminders just click the "Trash" button.

12. To delete an archived reminder simply drag it to the left.

13. To edit archived reminder open it or drag the item list to the right.

17. To enable the ability to save backups to cloud storage, go to Settings > Export and Sync > Third Party Service and in the window that appears, choose the appropriate cloud storage.
